pagecord.com/spiri is now spi.blue

I've always wanted to have my own domain name phrase hack that aligns with my semi-anonymous username, "spiritual blues."

I considered various options, such as spiritualblues.net and spiritualblues.org. Unfortunately, spiritualblues.com has been squatted on and is now listed on the aftermarket for $600. I thought about using spiri.fyi, but the .blue TLD just feels like a natural fit for my username. I also considered spiritualblu.es, although I worry that  SEO might take a hit globally. Another option was spiri.blog, but I'm uncertain if I will always want this pointed to a blog. The name could also serve as a domain for a landing page in the future.

Here are some domain name hacks from the past that I admired - 
  • del.icio.us
  • about.me/username
  • vacation.rentals
  • home.loans
  • simpl.fyi

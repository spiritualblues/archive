I am grateful for my family and job, but we all need a break from the relentless madness of the day. Audi must have been on to something when they launched this marketing campaign. If you identify with this feeling, try this: Pause for a few minutes when you park your car in the garage. No, don't open Instagram. Stay in your vehicle for a good five minutes before stepping out. It should be plenty to recharge you for the evening.

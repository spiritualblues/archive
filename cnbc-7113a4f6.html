CNBC +

CNBC has quietly launched a standaline streaming service for $15 per month. This includes their live CNBC stream and on demand library of daily episodes. 

Until now the cheapest way to get this channel was buying sling blue plus the news add-on which sets you back by 51$ per month. Which is a steep price if all you watch is CNBC. So this is a peculiar choice for a niche audience that want CNBC without all the sports and other news channels bundled in. 

Comcast plans to spin away MSNBC, CNBC and USA. I am curious to see who keeps the rights for EPL. I bet they would stay with peacock.

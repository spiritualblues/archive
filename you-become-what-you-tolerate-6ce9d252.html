"You become what you tolerate"

Specific individual quotes have influenced my work ethic and daily drive, playing a role in my formative years as I grew personally and professionally.  
One of them is the title above. To my disappointment, I have never found the original quote or author I could 100 percent confidently credit for this one. There are several variations that I can discover quoted in articles, but strangely, none of them are, as I always recollected the quote as above. This is the most original-sounding version I have found. 

“When it comes to standards, as a leader, it’s not what you preach, it’s what you tolerate.” — Leif Babin.
